Swave Pay

Swavepay is a payment card that allows for easy conversion of cryptocurrency to fiat currency. Developed by the Swave Network and issued by Walleto, it is powered by Visa or Mastercard and set to roll out in stages starting in Europe and expanding to the US, Canada & the rest of the world. It offers competitive fees and a transaction-based revenue model, making it a go-to solution for crypto-to-fiat transactions.

Swave Pulse

SwavePulse is a revolutionary app that makes buying and saving in Bitcoin easy and accessible for everyone. Our app is linked to your bank account, & auto-matically rounds up your purchases to the nearest dollar, using the difference to purchase Bitcoin. Our user-friendly interface and seamless integration with the Swave Exchange allows you to easily manage your savings and make Bitcoin purchases with just a few clicks.

Swave Block

SwaveBlock, the innovative property management software that aims to revolutionizing rent payments. With Swave Block, tenants can easily pay their rent and earn points that can be redeemed with our partner stores such as clothing stores, grocery stores, movie theaters, gyms, and restaurants. In addition, we offer the option to pay rent with Bitcoin, and incentivize our tenants to sign up to the Swaveplus, Swavepay, & Swave Exchange platforms. Make rent payment a rewarding experience with SwaveBlock.
